## Usage Notes

- **Open-source drawings:** Elemental released these explicitly for free use. Credit: "ELEMENTAL, Open-Source Housing Plans, elementalchile.cl"
- **ArchDaily / Dezeen photos:** These are copyrighted editorial images. For an academic assignment, fair use applies — cite the source and photographer.
- **Academic paper figures:** Figures from Carrasco & O'Brien (2021) are published under open-access terms — cite the paper.
- **Recommended minimum set for the PDF:**
  1. Architectural drawing (open-source from Elemental)
  2. Before photo (MoMA or Dezeen archive)
  3. After photo showing expansion (ArchDaily or Carrasco & O'Brien)
  4. Aerial/site plan (Carrasco & O'Brien Figure 1 or ArchDaily)
  5. Self-made value comparison chart (data table above)
